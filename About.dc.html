About Us

Our Story

From the coconut groves of Tangalle to growers worldwide — discover how we bring nature's finest growing medium to your farm.

Rooted in Tangalle

Ceylon Substrates was founded with a clear mission: to transform Sri Lanka's abundant coconut resources into world-class growing media. Based in Tangalle on the island's scenic southern coast, we're surrounded by some of the finest coconut groves in the country.

Every coconut husk that enters our facility is carefully processed, washed, and quality-tested to produce cocopeat, cocochips, blocks, and grow slabs that meet the exacting standards of professional growers across the globe.

We believe in sustainability at every step — our products are 100% natural, biodegradable, and made from a renewable by-product that would otherwise go to waste.
