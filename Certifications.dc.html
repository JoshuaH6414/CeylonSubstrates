Quality Control Process

Every batch goes through a rigorous multi-stage testing process before it leaves our facility.

01
Raw Material Check

Incoming coconut husks inspected for maturity, freshness, and contamination before processing.

02
EC & pH Testing

Electrical conductivity and pH levels measured on every batch to ensure they fall within optimal ranges.

03
Sieving & Grading

Cocopeat sieved through calibrated mesh screens to achieve precise particle sizes and remove impurities.

04
Moisture Analysis

Moisture content verified to ensure proper compression, storage stability, and consistent expansion ratios.

05
Final Inspection

Finished products inspected for weight, dimensions, packaging integrity, and labelling accuracy before shipping.
